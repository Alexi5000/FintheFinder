# Research As Product Data

Fin stores research as structured product data: sessions, sources, evaluations, learnings, claims, approvals, events, and reports. That choice makes the app slower to build than a chat box, but much easier to trust.

The core design rule is simple: a report cannot be ready just because a model wrote it. It becomes ready when its material claims map to known sources, contradiction and citation checks pass, gaps are handled, and a human can inspect the trail.

That is why the FDE build-out includes a claim ledger and approval state machine. They create the operational surface an analyst needs: not only an answer, but the evidence and decisions behind it.
